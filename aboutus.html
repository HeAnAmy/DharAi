Who we are?

We provide highly qualified professionals to support aerospace industry, employing experts in aircraft maintenance and repair. DHAR AIR has a dynamic culture focused on bringing together the right talent team to deliver solutions that help our customers across Canada, operate more efficiently.


Our leadership

The founders of DHAR AIR are actively working in Aircraft Maintenance, that makes us know the needs of the industry, the skills and knowledge that candidates must have in the respective specialties, this understanding sets us apart in our ability to meet industry demands effectively..

David Aguilar
Founder and Director

Under his leadership, DHAR AIR INC’s focus is to help our staff achieve their personal and professional goals, helping them feel empowered, respected a skilled, inclusive and diverse workforce, which is reflected in the service that we provide to our customers.


David Aguilar

David Aguilar has been provided maintenance, repair and overhaul services in the aeronautical industry for over 21 years, in Mexico, USA and Canada. Mr Aguilar holds a bachelor’s degree in Control and Automation Engineering,the AME M2 License and the FAA A&P License. His experience is based on performing mechanical troubleshooting and maintenance on heavy checks, inspections, modifications, testing, overhaul maintenance, A checks and C checks on ATR72, DASH-8, B737, B747, B757, B767, B787, A-320 family, A-330, Embraer 175, Embraer 190 aircraft. He worked from the bottom up starting as an apprentice Aircraft Mechanic and later he was promoted to Aircraft Team Leader, always focus to work efficiently and safely, providing technical solutions.

Rocio Bautista

Rocio Bautista holds a bachelor’s degree in Control and Automation engineering, a technical degree in Metrology and Quality Control, and the AME M2 License in Canada. Mechanic in Canada. Rocio has over 13 years of experience in aviation industry, she has got experience working in Avionics Systems on ATR72, DASH-8, B737, B747, B757, B767, B787, A-319, A-320, Embraer 175, Embraer 190, MD-11 and DC-9 Aircrafts. As Quality Control Inspector in a Metrology Laboratory, performing calibration and preventive maintenance on measuring instruments used in aircraft, also obtained training in Non-Destructive Eddy Current Testing Level 1.
