How we serve?

DHAR AIR is a strategic alliance for aircraft maintenance companies, we have the ability to provide highly qualified professionals, helping you to cover your needs, no matter the complex of the project.

Canada is a leader in the aeronautical industry worldwide, due to its high-quality standards and the continuous growth of this industry, the labour shortage is evident.

While aerospace schools are in the process of graduating young students to take on the aviation industry, we can simultaneously provide you with qualified and experienced staff. Who in turn will help the new graduates to face our industry.

To make sure the quality and reliability in the service, we make an intense selection process before we provide solutions for your staffing needs.
