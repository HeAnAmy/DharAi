Careers for international candidates

Discover the world of opportunities at DHAR AIR in Canada, where you can grow in an environment where learning is continuous. Be recognized and rewarded for the impact you make by taking the first step in your career path. Collaborate with our industry leaders and explore a variety of skill enhancement opportunities to make a positive difference for the clients we serve.


What can you do at DHAR AIR in Canada?

You can be the best version of yourself by sharing and putting into practice your knowledge in the aviation industry.


How to join us

Do the work you love with people who have your back. We'll help you make an impact from the moment you join.

If your qualifications meet the requirements for the position, you will be contacted by a member of our global recruitment team. Following the interview, the global recruiter will work with our workforce mobility team to discuss immigration options.

All interviews will be conducted virtually. Prior to your interview, you will receive details on how to download and access our virtual interview platform.

During your interview:
-Ensure you are in a quiet space
-Check that your audio and video capabilities are working
-Relax and be yourself!


How we support you

Immigrating and settling into Canada is an exciting time. We recognize it is also an experience that brings with it significant change, including understanding a new culture and adapting to new ways of living and working. To support you in this transition, DHAR AIR in Canada provides Newcomers with the tools to build your network and reach your full potential.


We guide you to obtain services, including, but not limited to, finding and securing accommodation, opening bank accounts, introduction to social organizations, orientation to the public transportation system, shopping locations, health care providers, etc.


DHAR AIR Community

You'll have the opportunity to join a community of newcomers who help mentor our partners to enable seamless integration. This community hosts information sessions on frequently asked topics, such as Canadian taxes, permanent residence and more...
